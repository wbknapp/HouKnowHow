HouKnowHow:
Our Purpose

HouKnowHow’s purpose is to make the citizens of Houston more aware of helpful information about their environment and the resources that are available to them.

This mobile friendly interactive website utilizes a common language and is tied to physical points throughout the community via art installations, murals, signage, and other media.
